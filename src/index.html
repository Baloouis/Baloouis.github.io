About me

My name is Damien Marillet and since I am a kid I’ve been passionate about art and science and everything that lies in between,
That’s why after getting a master’s degree in AI at Sorbonne Universités in Paris I finally decided to follow my passion and try to work in the video game industry.
With a colleague from university we started our own independent game studio called Big Catto Games
During our last year of master. We then developed DropDaBomb our published first game over several months, which is available on Google Play right now

Also working as a freelance AR programmer in collaboration with Atomic Digital Design

I get to work as part of a team specialized in AR developpement with 2D/3D artists and a director with a vision to follow.

Always on the lookout to keep growing and learning, I love to work with a team, take the time to learn from my colleagues, or support and help them whenever I can.
